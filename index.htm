Tom & Jerry

"Tom and Jerry" is a classic animated series created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. It features the comedic rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a clever mouse. Set in various locations, the show is known for its slapstick humor, elaborate chases, and clever schemes. Despite their constant battles, there are moments of friendship between Tom and Jerry. This timeless cartoon has entertained audiences of all ages for decades, making it one of the most beloved cartoons of all time.
